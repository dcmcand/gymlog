A simple, privacy-focused workout tracker for Android. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Just you and your lifts.

Features:

- Exercise library with weight and cardio exercise types
- Reusable workout templates with target sets, reps, weight, and distance
- Real-time workout tracking with Easy/Hard/Fail status per set
- Auto-adjusted weight suggestions based on previous performance and recency
- Exercise-level weight control to adjust all sets at once
- Quick weight adjustments with +/- 2.5 kg buttons
- Rest timer with 1:30 countdown and vibration alert
- Calendar view showing workout history by date
- Exercise progress charts for weight, distance, and time
- Resume in-progress workouts
- Fully offline - all data stored locally on your device
